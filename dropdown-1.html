Al-Barakat-ul-Makiyyah

Al-Barakat Al-Makkiyah Fi As-Salawat An-Nabawiyah is a distinguished collection that holds a special place in Islamic
literature, focusing on the blessings and virtues of sending salutations (Salawat) upon the Prophet Muhammad (peace be
upon him). This revered text, rooted in the spiritual traditions of Islam, provides a comprehensive compilation of various
forms of Durood Sharif and Salawat.

Durood Wa Salam

A path to peace and prosperity


Durood Sharif, a beautiful invocation of blessings upon the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), holds immense spiritual significance in Islam. Reciting Durood or Salawat brings countless rewards, fostering a deep connection with Allah and His beloved Prophet. It is said to invoke peace, mercy, and forgiveness, and carries the promise of blessings in both this world and the hereafter.
